THE PROBLEM

Pharma generates demand. But loses patients before they start therapy.

Intake drop-off

Patients abandon complex intake flows before reaching a provider.

Provider friction

HCPs lack the tools and prompts to initiate treatment efficiently.

PA delays

Prior authorization bottlenecks stall therapy starts for weeks.

LDD routing issues

Limited distribution drugs get lost between specialty pharmacies.

THE SOLUTION

Klinic turns demand into treatment starts.

We own the full funnel — from the moment a patient searches for treatment information to the moment they fill their first prescription.
