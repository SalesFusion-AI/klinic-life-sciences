The landscape today

Specialty care is harder to run than it should be.

Clinics and HCPs are asked to be growth teams, ops teams, compliance teams, and care teams at once. Something always slips.

Fragmented admin stack

Patients say yes but never convert. Referrals stall between the form and the first visit.

Prior auths eat clinician time

Hours a week per clinician lost to phone trees, payer portals, and rework on denied claims.

State-by-state compliance drag

Every new state means months of legal research, new SOPs, and credentialing before you can see a patient.
